# EarthVerse AI 🌍

Don’t just calculate your carbon footprint. Experience the future consequences of your choices.

## Overview

EarthVerse AI is a futuristic climate intelligence platform that transforms daily lifestyle habits into environmental legacy scores, future simulations, and AI-generated sustainability insights.

Unlike traditional carbon calculators, EarthVerse AI focuses on storytelling, behavioral analysis, and future climate simulation.

## Problem Statement

Most carbon footprint platforms only show numbers and charts.

Users fail to emotionally connect with the environmental consequences of their actions.

EarthVerse AI solves this by simulating future environmental outcomes and making climate awareness interactive.

## Features

- Climate Legacy Score
- AI Future Letter Generator
- Parallel Timeline Simulation
- Climate Ripple Effect Engine
- AI Climate Mentor Hub
- Climate Passport
